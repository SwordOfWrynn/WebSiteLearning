Some helpful sites with Web Safety tips

usa.kaspersky.com

Summary: Keep information professional and limited, keep privacy settings on, use secure internet connections, be careful what you post and download

webroot.com

Summary: Avoid questionable sites, only download from trusted sources, increase browser security settings, and don't use email links

getsafeonline.org

Summary: Check URLs for small misspellings, check link destinations before you click on them, ensure you are on a https site, and if you are suspicous of a site avoid it or do a web search on it
