Trucks

The backbone of any logistics effort, Trucks are used to quickly mobilize troops and supplies.

Warden Trucks

Dunne Transport

The Dunne Transport is the standard Warden logistics vehicle. Capable of transporting troops and material to and from the frontline, it is a critical part of the Warden war effort.


Dunne Loadlugger 3c

The Loadlugger 3c is a standard Warden Truck fitted with a resource hopper in place of the standard cargo hold. This allows for a much greater capacity for resources at the expense of space for cargo and troops.


Dunne Fuelrunner 2d

The Fuelrunner 2d is a modified Warden Truck designed to transport and distribute large quantities of fuel.


Dunne Landrunner 12c

The Landrunner 12c is a standard Warden Truck fitted with rugged off-road treads, allowing for more efficient movement on rough terrain and conditions at the expense of maximum speed.


Dunne Leatherback 2a

The Leatherback 2a is a heavy reinforced Dunne Transport. Fitted with a heavier frame and a more armoured drivers compartment, it is capable of enduring more punishment at the cost of initial acceleration.


Colonial Trucks

R-1 Hauler

The R-1 Hauler is the standard Colonial logistics vehicle. Capable of transporting troops and material to and from the frontline, it is a critical part of the Colonial war machine.


R-5 "Atlas" Hauler

The R-5 "Atlas" Hauler is a standard Colonial Truck fitted with a resource hopper in place of the standard cargo hold. This allows for a much greater capacity for resources at the expense of space for cargo and troops.


RR-3 "Stolon" Tanker

The RR-3 "Stolon" Tanker is a modified Colonial Truck designed to transport and distribute large quantities of fuel.


R-5b "Sisyphus" Hauler

The R-5b "Sisyphus" is a R-5 Hauler fitted with an improved suspension and axle system resulting in better overall handling. However these improvements may not hold up under sever weather conditions.


R-9 "Speartip" Escort

The R-9 "Speartip" Escort is a Colonial Truck fitted with a Light Machinegun in place of the passenger seat. It's well suited as an escort for convoys or lightly armoured operations.
