Light Tanks

Light Tanks are the most versatile standard tanks in the game, having good speed and decent firepower.

Warden Light Tanks

Devitt Mk. III

The standard Warden Light Tank, the Devitt Mk. III is a highly maneuverable lightweight tank. Designed for urban environments, it is fitted with a 40mm cannon. Whether it's on the front line, protecting a convoy, or leading a border assault, the Devitt is a sturdy and reliable asset.


Devitt Ironhide Mk. IV

A more sturdy Warden Light Tank, The Ironhide is similar to the Mk. III but reinforced with plates of heavy steel at the expense of it's speed and maneuverability.


Colonial Light Tanks

H5 "Hatchet"

The standard Colonial Light Tank, The H5, or "Hatchet", is a versatile light tank capable of navigating through tight mountainous regions or urban streets and is fitted with a 40mm cannon.


H-8 "Kranesca"

The "Kranesca" Light Tank is a H-class tank fitted with an overpowered engine and a reinforced chassis, capable of boosting its top speed at the expense of overall acceleration and maneuverability


H-10 "Pelekys"

The "Pelekys" H-class Light Tank is heavily modified with an open top chassis and equipped with a devastating long-range anti-tank cannon
