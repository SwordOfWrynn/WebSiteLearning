Armored Cars

Armoured Cars are quick, well-rounded urban assault platforms.

Warden Armored Cars

O'Brien v.110

The standard Warden Armored Car, this anti-infantry vehicle is equipped with twin barrelled machineguns. The O'Brien v.110 is capable on the frontline, and excels at logistics escort, or patrolling vulnerable supply routes.


O'Brien v.121 Highlander

Fitted with all-terrain tread, the O'Brien Highlander brings significant all-terrain mobility and performs especially well in snowy and mountainous environments.

Colonial Armored Cars

T3 "Xiphos"

The standard Colonial Armored Car, this anti-infantry vehicle is equipped with twin barrelled machineguns. The T3 "Xiphos" is capable on the frontline, and excels at logistics escort, or patrolling vulnerable supply routes.


T5 "Percutio"

The "Percutio" Armored Car variant is fitted with a high-powered anti-tank rifle turret in place of the twin machine gun platform.


T12 "Actaeon" Tankette

The T12 "Actaeon" is a complete overhaul of the T3 Armoured Car reinforced with tank armour. While these extra defenses lower the T12's overall speed and handling, the addition of treads provides increased performance in less than ideal terrain.
