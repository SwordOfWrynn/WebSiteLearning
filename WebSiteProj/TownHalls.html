Town Halls

The political center of a town or region, Town Halls are a symbolic and strategic target.

The Town Hall is the most important structure in a town, as it allows players to spawn, provides a stockpile for equipment, garrisons AI structures in a large radius, and control of the Town Hall is required to build the other structures of the town. There are three tiers of Town Hall.


Garrison Camp

The temporary base of a new administration, the Garrison Camp is little more than a tent giving the occupiers a place to oversee the town or city.

The Garrison Camp is the first tier of Town Hall, and has the least amount of health, and costs the most to build. Like all Tier 1 structures, it can be damaged by heavy weapons.


Garrison Base

The base of an established administration, the Garrison Base allows its owners to further control the town it oversees.

The Garrison Base is the second tier of Town Hall, and allows the construction of Tier 2 buildings in the town.


Town Hall

The base of a well entrenched administration, the Town Hall signifies the complete control of the town.

The Town Hall is the final tier, and has the most health. It allows the construction of Tier 3 buildings in the town.
