Assault Tanks

Assault Tanks are the medium standard tanks, having either more armor or firepower than Light Tanks, but more mobility than Battle Tanks.

Warden Assault Tanks

Silverhand-Mk. IV

The Silverhand assault tank is fitted with destructive dual barrel armaments, and heavy frontal and rear armour. Its 68mm frontal cannon is paired with a lighter 40mm turret.
