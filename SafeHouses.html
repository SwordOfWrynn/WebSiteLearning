Safe Houses

A well fortified house, the Safe House serves as a backup center of operations for towns under siege..

The Safe House is a special type of Garrison House that also serves as a forward base for players to spawn at. Safe Houses are critical part of a towns defenses, either providing a significant obstacle to the enemy forces trying to reach the Town Hall, or providing a base to launch counter strikes from if the Town Hall falls. The Safe House provides AI in a medium radius, and allows the controlling faction to rebuild nearby Garrison Houses, even if they have lost control of the Town Hall. A fully upgraded Safe House with Artillery Shelter can be very hard to kill if properly supplied and defended, as (for the most part) it can only be killed from the inside. The Safe House has far more health than other Garrison Houses, and has 3 Tiers, each having more health, with Tier 3 being able to shoot Anti-Tank Rifles at enemy vehicles.
