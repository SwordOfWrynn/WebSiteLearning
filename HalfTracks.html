Half-tracks

The Half-track is a versatile combat weapons platform that can serve many roles on the battlefield.

Warden Half-tracks

Niska Mk. 1 Gun Motor Carriage

The base Warden Half-track, the Niska Mk. 1 is a heavily armoured versatile all-terrain vehicle with a mounted machine gun, designed for escort missions and to support infantry operations.


Niska Mk. 2 Blinder

A Warden Half-track specialized in the anti-tank role, the Niska Mk. 2 is fitted with a heavy-duty anti-tank gun capable of punching through all but the most tempered of alloys.


Colonial Half-tracks

HH-a "Javelin"

The base Colonial Half-track, the HH-a "Javelin" is a lightly armoured versatile all-terrain vehicle with a mounted machine gun, designed for escort missions and to support infantry operations.


HH-b "Hoplite"

A more sturdy design on the Colonial Half-track, the HH-b "Hoplite" has been given reinforced armour at the expense of speed, making it a formidable force in the heat of combat.


HH-d Peltast

The HH-d "Peltast" Half-Track variant is fitted with a 360 degree mortar platform, capable of providing mobile artillery support to the infantry in frontline operations.
